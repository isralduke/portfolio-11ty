Just in Time Usability

Isral Duke presents why accessibility is important for business. He also presents 10 things that site owners can do to quick check their site’s accessibility and usability. Presented at a local ecommerce group lunch.

Accessible, Friendly Content

Isral Duke demonstrates how iOS and MacOS VoiceOver presents a simple web page. This talk promotes the use of semantic, meaningful markup to make a better user experience. Presented at Google Developer Groups.
